What is Vocarchive?

Vocarchive it is a space created for combine voicebanks from all platforms, from Vocaloid to UTAU and more. Our purpose is to connect people with new voices, give them a place to discover and share music, and form ahub of creativity and community. Every submission, every voice shared, helps us grow and allows more people to find inspiration in new voices. Be part of this project and help keep the magic of voices alive!
