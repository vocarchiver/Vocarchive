What is Vocarchive?

Vocarchive is a centralized catalog of voicebanks and vocal synthesis software across all platforms â€” from Vocaloid and Synthesizer V to UTAU and beyond.

Our mission is to preserve, document, and give equal visibility to voices from creators of all sizes, while providing a reliable place to discover both new and historical voicebanks.

Every submission helps grow the archive and keeps the history of vocal synthesis alive.

Demo Resources

To ensure consistency and fairness across all submissions, Vocarchive provides official MIDI templates that you can use to create your voicebank demos.

  • These MIDIs help standardize demos so voices can be compared more accurately.
  • You are encouraged (but not required) to use them.
  • All demos must still follow the demo format rules listed in the Guidelines.
